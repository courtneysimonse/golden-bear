This project began about three years ago, sparked by my curiosity about how often the training ship visits Australiaâ€”and what goes into planning those port calls. Despite searching across official school websites, I couldn't find much information, let alone any visualizations. As a visual thinker, I was surprised there wasn't already a map showing this kind of data. Sure, there were some records from the past few years, but they were limited and scattered.

I decided it was time to change that.

With the help of a friend studying GIS, I discovered ArtemisMAPs and decided to fund a small project to bring this idea to life. What you see here is the result. I hope it helps shed light on these voyages in a way that's both accessible and engaging.
